Current Community Issues

Countryside

Florin has always known for its beautiful countryside in which many great and historical events have occurred. However, with a lack of maintanence much of the local landscape is becoming unrecogniseable. We need volunteers to help maintain and care for our landscape.

Library

The library has been a pinnacle in the community for years providing access to valuable knowledge. More recently, we have encountered staffing shortages which may result in closing library. If you're interested in helping take a look at he volunteering page.

Recycling

Our recycling center is bursting with potential, a beacon of sustainability in our community. As the demand for responsible waste management grows, we invite you to join us in the mission to educate and efficiently manage our recycling facility. Volunteer to make a lasting impact, shaping a greener future for generations to come. Together, let's transform our overflowing center into a hub of environmental consciousness and positive change.

Skills Shortage

Generations of wisdom, skills passed down. Today, a shortage looms. Be the link. Volunteer to teach, preserve our community's legacy!
